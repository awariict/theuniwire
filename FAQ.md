# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is TheUniWire?
TheUniWire is a professional news and content management platform designed specifically for Nigerian universities to share news, events, and announcements.

### Who can use TheUniWire?
Students, staff, and alumni of Nigerian universities can read articles. Universities can register to publish their own content.

### Is TheUniWire free?
Yes, TheUniWire is free for readers. Universities may need to register for content publishing.

## Technical Questions

### What technologies does TheUniWire use?
Next.js 14, React 18, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, MongoDB, NextAuth.js, and more.

### Can I contribute to TheUniWire?
Yes! We welcome contributions. Please fork the repository and submit pull requests.

### How do I report bugs?
Please use our GitHub issues page: https://github.com/awariict/theuniwire/issues

## User Account

### How do I create an account?
Click "Sign Up" and fill in your details. Verify your email to activate your account.

### How do I change my password?
Go to account settings and click "Change Password".

### How do I delete my account?
Contact support at support@theuniwire.ng to request account deletion.

## Content

### How do I publish an article?
As an editor or above, go to the admin dashboard and click "Create Article". Fill in the details and submit for approval.

### How long does article approval take?
Typically 24-48 hours depending on admin availability.

### Can I edit my published article?
Editors can edit their own articles before publication. Contact admins for published articles.

### How do I add images to articles?
Use the image button in the article editor or drag and drop images directly.

## Comments

### Can I comment on articles?
Yes, registered users can comment on articles.

### Are comments moderated?
Yes, comments are reviewed before publication to maintain community standards.

### Can I delete my comments?
Yes, you can delete your own comments anytime.

## Search & Discovery

### How do I search for articles?
Use the search bar at the top of the page or go to the Articles section.

### Can I filter by category?
Yes, click on a category to view articles in that category.

### Can I save articles?
Yes, click the bookmark icon to save articles for later.

## Notifications

### How do I get notifications?
Go to settings and enable notifications you're interested in.

### Can I unsubscribe from emails?
Yes, click "Unsubscribe" in any email or manage preferences in settings.

## University Registration

### How do I register my university?
Contact admin at admin@theuniwire.ng with your university details.

### What information do I need to provide?
University name, location, website, logo, and main contact information.

## Privacy & Security

### Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security practices.

### What data do you collect?
We collect name, email, and usage data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

### Can I opt out of data collection?
You can manage your privacy settings, but basic account data is required.

## Troubleshooting

### I'm having login issues
Clear your browser cache and try again. If the problem persists, use "Forgot Password".

### Images aren't loading
Check your internet connection. Try refreshing the page or clearing cache.

### The website is slow
Try:
- Clearing browser cache
- Disabling browser extensions
- Using a different browser
- Checking your internet speed

### I found a bug
Please report it on GitHub or email us at support@theuniwire.ng
